What We Offer

  • Freedom to research and innovate on any open source technology.
  • End-to-end software development support (ideation to deployment).
  • Guidance from experienced mentors and industry professionals.
  • Recognition and certificate on successful contribution/release.
  • Opportunity to feature your project in our open source showcase.

How It Works

Step 1: Apply

Submit your open source idea or choose from our list of active project proposals.

Step 2: Research

Study the problem, identify technologies, and outline your development approach.

Step 3: Develop

Code, test, and collaborate with mentors while building your open source solution.

Step 4: Publish

Release your work on GitHub, get recognized, and inspire future developers.
